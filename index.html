Logo Color & Typography Icons
Oxford Blue: Used for the menu, top and bottom navigation bar. We have picked this shade of blue because of its sense of reliability and trust
Tufts Blue: Used for the buttons throughout the website. It is a trustworthy color and it offsests the dark tone of the menu.
Mint Green: Used to compliment the different shades of blue and it gives a softer feeling which can bring a uniqueness to the visual aspect of the design
White: Used for the background color to provide good visibility for users and also providing a good contrast with the shades of blue and green.
